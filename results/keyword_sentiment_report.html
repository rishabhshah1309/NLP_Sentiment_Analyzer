üéØ Keyword Scoring Methodology

Multi-Method Scoring Approach

Each keyword receives a sentiment score based on multiple analysis methods:

  • Custom Sports Lexicon (40%): Domain-specific sentiment scores for sports/sponsorship terms
  • VADER Lexicon (30%): Social media optimized sentiment scores
  • Context Analysis (30%): Sentiment of surrounding words and phrases

Confidence Scoring

Confidence levels indicate reliability of sentiment scores:

  • High Confidence (0.7+): Word appears 7+ times, consistent sentiment pattern
  • Medium Confidence (0.4-0.7): Word appears 4-6 times, mostly consistent
  • Low Confidence (<0.4): Word appears 2-3 times, may have mixed usage

Score Interpretation

  • +1.5 to +2.0: Very strong positive sentiment (amazing, incredible, perfect)
  • +0.5 to +1.4: Positive sentiment (good, great, excited, smart)
  • -0.4 to +0.4: Neutral/factual (announced, deal, contract, million)
  • -1.4 to -0.5: Negative sentiment (bad, disappointing, waste)
  • -2.0 to -1.5: Very strong negative sentiment (disgusting, betrayal, outrageous)
